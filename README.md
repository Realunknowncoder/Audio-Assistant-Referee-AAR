# 📦 AAR (Audio Assistant Referee)

> *No more "I swear I didn't say it" or "I told you earlier"*


<img width="1748" height="2480" alt="poster_AAR" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/8fcd2bf0-5f2e-433c-ab6b-b9cad8b29309" />



## 🌟 Features

- Record up to 1h and 30 minutes
- Build in microphone and speaker
- Upgradable storage (because that's a plus in our days :[ )


## ℹ️ Overview

Hi, I'm Yassine Moustarziq and I am tired of those people who sware they never said this or that they informed me at the start of the conversation that he did say it, and I know we all have that one guy either your teacher, friend, sibling, parents! But fear no more, for I present to you the AAR (Audio Asssitant Referee), the perfect recorder of discussion to make sure you can always come back to that exact moment, I mean we have VAR (Video Assistant Referee) for video fotage, but what about audio? Well now we do, so let's solve this problem together with AAR!
